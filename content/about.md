I'm James Dreben, a software engineer based in **San Juan, Puerto Rico**. For roughly a decade I've been building software at the intersection of artificial intelligence, site reliability, and full-stack web development — at startups, at scale, and at a few places in between.

I think of myself as a builder first. I care about systems that work, code that's easy to reason about, and teams that can move fast without flinching at 3am pages. I believe the best engineers are also good writers, good communicators, and perpetually curious people.

I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from **Harvard University** (Class of 2017), where my coursework included machine learning, algorithms, systems, and reinforcement learning.

## Where I've Worked

**Red Ventures** — Software Engineer *(current)*
Full-stack engineering, AI tooling, and publisher technology across the WP Engine ecosystem.

**Zoba → acquired by Marti** — Founding Engineer → CTO *(2017–2024)*
Co-founded Zoba, a machine learning company that predicted human mobility patterns in cities to optimize micromobility fleet placement. Grew from first engineer to CTO. Zoba was acquired by [Marti](https://marti.tech) in February 2024.

**Revenue intelligence startup** — Software Engineer
B2B SaaS for product-led growth. Built pipeline visibility tools, product analytics infrastructure, and dashboards that gave sales teams real signal from behavioral data.

**Virtual-first workspace company** — Software Engineer
Async-first remote work tooling. Built real-time collaboration infrastructure, presence systems, and integrations that made distributed teams feel less distributed.

**AI application startup** — Software Engineer
Applied AI and LLM tooling before it became fashionable. Built the product and the systems around it — inference pipelines, evaluation frameworks, and user-facing features.

**Quorum** — Productivity Technology Lead
Internal tooling and developer workflow automation. The kind of invisible work that makes everyone else faster.

**Lockheed Martin** — Technology Intern
Defense systems engineering.

**Deloitte** — Business Technology Scholar
Enterprise digital transformation consulting.
